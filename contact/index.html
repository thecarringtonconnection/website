Getting to the Grove Park Office


By Car or Bicycle

The Grove Park office is located off Baring Road, Lewisham (SE12).

Free, non-restricted, on-street parking is available. Secure bicycle parking is also available.


By Bus

Bus stops along routes 261, 273, and 284 are within walking distance.


By Train

The Grove Park office is located in between Grove Park train station (approx. 12-minute walk) and Lee train station (approx. 20-minute walk).
